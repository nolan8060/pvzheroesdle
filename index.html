About PvZ Heroes Wordle

How to Play

Guess the PvZ Heroes card in as few tries as possible!

  • Green = Correct attribute
  • Yellow = Partially correct (for tribes/traits)
  • Red = Incorrect attribute

Game Modes

Classic: Guess from all cards (Plants & Zombies)

Plants: Guess only from Plant cards

Zombies: Guess only from Zombie cards

Endless: Play unlimited random cards - no daily limit!

Hard Mode

When enabled, any revealed hints (green or yellow) must be used in all subsequent guesses.
